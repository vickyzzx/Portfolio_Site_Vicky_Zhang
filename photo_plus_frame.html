Challenges

When designing for both IOS and Android systems, keeping the design consistent could be difficult. The difference in user habits between IOS and Android was huge. During the design process, I carefully followed the design guideline and make the function design between the two systems the same use.

What I Have Learned

As a designer, I learned to create different versions of one screen helped me to learn and explore more possibilities in design.

From the users test feedback, I learned the details of the prototype were important, for getting real user feedback, the design should simulate the prototype to make it close to the real app.
